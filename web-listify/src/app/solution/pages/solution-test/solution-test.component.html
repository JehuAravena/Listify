Listify is the ultimate solution for individuals facing the challenge of ADHD (Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder). Our task management application is specifically designed for young adults aged 18 to 29 with this condition. Listify incorporates a gamification module in the frontend that enhances motivation and commitment in managing daily tasks. With an intuitive and attractive user interface, we focus on usability and the user experience, simplifying and improving the planning and organization of daily activities.
